Automatically tweets title and link to newly published articles on any WordPress blog.

== Description ==

Tweetster is an Omeka and WordPress plugin that allows you to easily and automatically have new additions to your Omeka archive or WordPress blog tweeted out to your Twitter audience. This helps generate traffic to your site and keeps followers updated on what you are currently working on. 

Tweetster is simple and efficient, and takes only a few minutes to set up. Here's what you'll need to get started with Tweetster:
* WordPress 3.0 or higher; or Omeka version 1.0 or higher
* Twitter Account
* Bit.ly Account

More information, and installation guide available on http://corybohon.com/tweetster/
